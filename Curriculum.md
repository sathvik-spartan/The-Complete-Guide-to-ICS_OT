# Long-Term Vision

This curriculum is intended to evolve into a complete, vendor-neutral, open-source learning platform for Operational Technology (OT) and Industrial Control Systems (ICS) cybersecurity. Each module will eventually become a standalone learning guide containing curated resources, official documentation, hands-on laboratories, real-world projects, self-assessments, interview preparation, and community contributions.
